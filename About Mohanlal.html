The Feudal Lord
Mohanlal Viswanathan

Mohanlal is a highly acclaimed Indian actor, producer, and singer, best known for his work in Malayalam cinema and widely referred to as "The Complete Actor". He has starred in over 400 films, won five National Film Awards and numerous Kerala State Film Awards, and received India's civilian honors of Padma Shri (2001) and Padma Bhushan (2019).

Dadasaheb Phalke Award(2025)

Malayalam actor Mohanlal received the Dadasaheb Phalke Award in 2025 for his lifetime contribution to Indian cinema. He was presented with the award by President Droupadi Murmu at the 71st National Film Awards ceremony in New Delhi on September 23, 2025. Mohanlal is the second recipient from Kerala to receive this honor.
